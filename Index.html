I'm a multimedia designer specializing in graphic and web design. I create visually engaging and user-focused designs that bring ideas to life. Beyond design, I also work in photography and videography, capturing and producing content that tells compelling stories.

With a strong eye for detail and creativity, I handle the full creative process from concept and design to shooting and editing. I’m proficient in industry-standard tools including Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe XD, Premiere Pro, as well as Figma and Canva, allowing me to deliver high-quality work across different platforms.

My goal is simple: to create visuals that not only look great, but also connect, communicate, and leave a lasting impression.
